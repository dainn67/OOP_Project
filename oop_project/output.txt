Ky Hong Bang Thi: Theo truyen thuyet thi thuy to dan toc Viet Nam la Kinh Duong Vuong, hien con co mo tai lang An Lu, Thuan Thanh, Bac Ninh.
Kinh Duong Vuong lam Vua vao khoang nam Nham Tuat (hon 2000 nam truoc Cong nguyen). Kinh Duong Vuong lay con gai Than Long la Vua ho Dong Dinh sinh mot con trai dat ten la Sung Lam, sau noi ngoi vua cha nien hieu la Lac Long Quan. Lac Long Quan lay con gai De Lai la Au Co sinh ra mot boc mot tram trung, mot tram trung no ra mot tram nguoi con trai la to tien cua nguoi Bach Viet.
Mot hom nha Vua bao ba Au Co: "Ta la giong Rong, nang la giong Tien, thuy hoa khac nhau, chung hop that kho". Ben tu biet, chia nam chuc nguoi con theo me len nui , nam chuc nguoi con theo cha ve phia Nam mien bien, phong cho con truong lam Hung Vuong noi ngoi.
Hung Vuong len ngoi Vua, dat quoc hieu la Van Lang, dong do o Phong Chau (Bach Hac, Phu Tho), chia nuoc ra lam 15 bo.
Cac doi Vua sau deu goi la Hung Vuong, co 18 doi vua Hung Vuong(*) . Dat cac tuong van goi la Lac Hau, tuong vo goi la Lac Tuong, con trai vua goi la Quan Lang, con gai Vua goi la My Nuong, cac quan nho goi la Bo Chinh.
Nha nuoc Van Lang cua cac Vua Hung la nha nuoc phoi thai dau tien cua Viet Nam, con rat don gian, mac du moi hinh thanh nhung da co ket duoc long nguoi. Tu tinh cam cong dong dan den y thuc cong dong, the hien ro tinh dong bao ruot thit. Ho buoc dau hieu duoc moi quan he giua thien nhien va con nguoi, thay duoc suc manh cua cong dong trong viec lam thuy loi, trao doi san pham va dau tranh giu gin ban lang, dat nuoc.
Thoi dai Hung Vuong co hai truyen thuyet duoc loan truyen mai trong dan gian the hien ro tinh than doan ket cua cong dong cac dan toc Viet.

Ta tuong Trinh Tung cung voi trieu than lap con thu 5 cua Le Anh Tong la Duy Dam len ngoi vua, va sai Nguyen Huu Lien di don vua Le Anh Tong, khi ve den Loi Duong - Thanh Hoa, ngam buc hai vua roi phao tin la vua tu that co.
Nhu vay Le Anh Tong o ngoi duoc16 nam, tho 42 tuoi.
Chi tiet: Le Anh Tong - Le Duy Bang

Le The Tong - Le Duy Dam: Le The Tong (1573-1599) ten huy la Duy Dam, sinh thang 11/1567. Thang 1/1573 duoc lap lam vua khi moi 7 tuoi, quyen hanh tat ca o trong tay ta tuong Trinh Tung.
Sau gan 50 nam noi chien Nam - Bac Trieu voi gan 40 tran danh lon nho, hang van dan lanh bi bat vao linh, phuc vu cho cac cuoc tan sat khung khiep. Co tran moi ben huy dong hang chuc van quan, hai ben giang co kha quyet liet, mai den nam 1591, Trinh Tung huy dong tong luc danh tran quyet dinh o Dong Kinh, thang 11/1592 bat duoc Mac Mau Hop, chiem duoc kinh thanh. Thang 2/1593, Trinh Tung don Le The Tong ve kinh do Dong Do.

Ngay 24 thang Tam nam Ky Hoi - 1599, Le The Tong mat, o ngoi duoc 26 nam, tho 33 tuoi.
Chi tiet: Le The Tong - Le Duy Dam

Next
Mac Thai To - Mac Dang Dung: Mac Dang Dung (1527-1529)
Mac Dang Dung que o lang Co Trai, huyen Nghi Duong (Hai Duong) la chau bay doi cua trang nguyen Mac Dinh Chi lam quan den chuc Nhap noi hanh khien (Te tuong) thoi Tran, bo la Mac Hich, me la Dang Thi Hien. Ong ba Mac Hich - Dang Thi Hien sinh duoc 3 nguoi con trai la: Mac Dang Dung, Mac Dang Doc va Mac Dang Quyet.
Mac Dang Dung sinh ngay 23 thang 11 nam Quy Mao - 1483. Thoi tre, Mac Dang Dung co suc khoe phi thuong, tuong mao khoi ngo. Ong xuat than tu mot thanh nien ngheo, lam nghe danh ca, trong mot dip di thi vo o kinh do da trung Do luc si va duoc sung vao Chau tuc ve chuyen cam du di theo vua.
Mac Dang Dung tien rat nhanh tren con duong hoan lo, nam 1511 moi 29 tuoi da duoc phong tuoc Vu xuyen ba. Nam 1516, Mac Dang Dung duoc cu lam Tran thu Son Nam voi chuc Pho tuong ta do doc.
Trai qua 3 doi vua Le, Mac Dang Dung duoc phong Thai su Nhan quoc cong roi den An hung vuong.
Loi dung luc vua Le Cung Hoang uon hen, thang 6 nam 1527, Mac Dang Dung tu Co Trai dem quan ve kinh do ep vua nhuong ngoi, lap len trieu Mac.
Cung nhu nha Tran, thang 12 nam 1529, Mac Dang Dung nhuong ngoi cho con la Mac Dang Doanh lam vua, con minh lam Thai thuong hoang. Luc do Mac Dang Dung moi 46 tuoi. Mac Dang Dung mat thang 8 nam Tan Suu - 1541, tho 59 tuoi.

Next
Mac Thai Tong - Mac Dang Doanh: Mac Dang Doanh (1530-1540)
Mac Dang Doanh la con truong cua Mac Dang Dung. Thang Gieng nam 1530 Mac Dang Doanh len ngoi vua, doi nien hieu la Dai Chinh, ton cha lam Thai thuong hoang.
Mac Dang Doanh lam cung dien nguy nga o Co Trai de Thai thuong hoang song o do vui thu dien vien, nhung ngu y la tran giu mot vung quan trong lam ngoai vien cho Mac Dang Doanh va van dinh doat nhung viec trong dai cua quoc gia.
Tu khi Mac Dang Doanh len ngoi vua thi o Thanh Hoa, cuu than nha Le la Nguyen Kim dua vao rung nui o bien gioi Viet - Lao lanh dao luc luong trung hung nha Le ngay cang lon manh. Nam Quy Ty - 1533, cac cuu than nha Le lap Le Duy Ninh len lam vua goi la Le Trang Tong.
Duoi trieu nha Mac, cu 3 nam mo mot ky thi Hoi, thi Dinh de tuyen chon nhan tai. Nguyen Binh Khiem do trang Nguyen duoi trieu Mac Dang Doanh.
Ngay 15 thang Gieng nam Canh Ty - 1540, Mac Dang Doanh chet, o ngoi duoc 10 nam.

Next
Mac Hien Tong - Mac Phuc Hai: Mac Phuc Hai (1541-1546)
Mac Phuc Hai la con truong cua Mac Dang Doanh, duoc ong noi la Thai thuong hoang Mac Dang Dung lap lam vua vao nam Tan Suu - 1541.
Thoi Mac Phuc Hai da tien hanh chia cap loc dien, dac biet uu tien binh si, vi luc luong quan si to lon duoc nuoi duong de chong lai nha Le trung hung (Nam Trieu).
Ngay 8 thang 5 nam Binh Ngo - 1546, Mac Phuc Hai chet, o ngoi vua duoc 5 nam. Con truong la Mac Phuc Nguyen ke vi.

Mac Tuyen Tong - Mac Phuc Nguyen: Mac Phuc Nguyen (1546-1561)
Mac Phuc Nguyen len ngoi vua vao thang 5 nam 1546, luc do con nho tuoi, moi cong viec trieu chinh deu do chu ruot la Khiem vuong Mac Kinh Dien quyet doan ca.
Thang 7/1557, Mac Phuc Nguyen sai Mac Kinh Dien dem quan vao danh Thanh Hoa. Quan Mac thua to, Mac Kinh Dien phai nhay xuong song an nap suot 3 ngay moi thoat chet.
Den nam Ky Mui - 1559, quan Le - Trinh mo cuoc tan cong vao hau phuong cua nha Mac o Son Tay, Tuyen Quang, Hung Hoa, Kinh Bac, Hai Duong... Mac Phuc Nguyen phai rut vao phong thu o ngoai thanh Dong Do.
Thang 12/1561, giua luc cuoc chien Le - Mac dang gay go quyet liet nhat thi Mac Phuc Nguyen chet vi benh dau mua. Mac Phuc Nguyen lam vua duoc 15 nam.

Mac Mau Hop: Mac Mau Hop (1562-1592)
Mac Mau Hop la con ca cua Mac Phuc Nguyen, nam 1562 len ngoi vua hay con be, ung vuong Mac Dang Nhuong (con ut Mac Dang Dung) lam Nhap noi phu chinh am Mac Mau Hop ra coi chau, ton ong chu la Khiem vuong Mac Kinh Dien lam Khiem dai vuong cung trong coi viec trieu chinh.
Ngay 21 thang 2 nam Mau Dan - 1578, Mac Mau Hop bi set danh o trong cung, liet nua nguoi, sau chua thuoc lai khoi, ben doi nien hieu la Dien Khanh.
Thang 10 nam Canh Thin - 1580, Phu chinh Mac Kinh Dien, tru cot cua trieu Mac chet, ung vuong Mac Dang Nhuong giu quyen phu chinh quyet dinh moi viec nhung lai thuong ve song o Duong Kinh vi vay viec trieu chinh be boi khong ai quyet doan.
Nam 1581, Mac Mau Hop bi chung thong manh, chua mai moi khoi. Khoi benh, Mac Mau Hop lao vao an choi truy lac. Chinh su nha Mac ngay cang do nat, binh luc suy yeu, long nguoi ly tan.
Ngay 25 thang 11 nam 1592, thuy quan Le - Trinh gom 300 chien thuyen danh vao cac huyen Kim Thanh, Thanh Ha, Nam Sach, Kinh Mon (tinh Hai Duong ngay nay). Quan Mac tan vo, du Dang nha Mac xin hang Trinh Tung rat dong. Mac Mau Hop chay tron, bi bat giai ve kinh do Dong Do, bi treo song 3 ngay roi bi chem dau o bai cat Bo De. Mac Mau Hop o ngoi vua duoc 30 nam, khi chet 31 tuoi.
Con trai Mac Mau Hop la Mac Toan chay tron, sau bi quan Trinh bat duoc dem chem dau tai ben Thao Tan.
Ho Mac tu Mac Dang Dung den Mac Mau Hop truyen ngoi duoc 5 doi thi mat, tong cong duoc 65 nam.
Con chau nha Mac theo loi day cua Trang Trinh Nguyen Binh Khiem rut len Cao bang, con keo dai duoc den nam 1677 moi bi diet han:
Mac Toan (1592-1592)
Mac Kinh Chi (1592-1593)
Mac Kinh Cung (1593-1625)
Mac Kinh Khoan (1623-1625)
Mac Kinh Vu (1638 - 1677)
Nhu vay nha Mac ton tai dung 150 nam.

Le Kinh Tong - Le Duy Tan: Le Kinh Tong (1600-1619) ten huy la Duy Tan, con thu cua Le The Tong, ngay 27 thang Tam nam Ky Hoi - 1599 duoc Trinh Tung lap lam vua, khi do moi 11 tuoi.
Tu dau the ky 17, sau khi danh tan nha Mac, gianh lai duoc kinh do Dong Do (du dang nha Mac phai rut len Cao Bang) quyen luc cua Trinh Tung ngay cang lon, trieu dinh chi biet phuc vu nha chua.
Truoc tinh hinh do, vua Le Kinh Tong cung voi Trinh Xuan - con thu cua Trinh Tung - muu giet Trinh Tung. Viec bai lo, Trinh Xuan bi bat giam, con Kinh Tong bi buc that co chet vao ngay 12 thang Nam nam Ky Mui - 1619.
Le Kinh Tong o ngoi duoc 19 nam, tho 32 tuoi.

Le Than Tong - Le Duy Ky: Le Than Tong (1619-1643 va 1649-1662) ten huy la Duy Ky, con truong cua Le Kinh Tong va ba Trinh Thi Ngoc Trinh (con thu cua Trinh Tung).

Le Hy Tong - Le Duy Hop: Le Hy Tong (1675-1705)
Le Hy Tong ten huy la Duy Hop, con thu cua Le Than Tong, em cua Le Gia Tong, duoc Tay vuong Trinh Tac lap lam vua luc moi 13 tuoi.

Thang 4 nam At Dau - 1705, nhuong ngoi cho con truong la Le Duy Duong. Vua Le Hy Tong con vui song trong canh nhan 12 nam sau moi mat, o ngoi duoc 30 nam, tho 54 tuoi.

Le Du Tong - Le Duy Duong: Le Du Tong (1705-1729)
Le Du Tong ten huy la Duy Duong, con truong cua Le Hy Tong, duoc len ngoi vua nam 1705.

Ho dua ra chu truong cai cach tren nhieu linh vuc ve kinh te - tai chinh, thi cu, to chuc hanh chinh... nhung cac cai cach do chua thu duoc ket qua thi chua Trinh Cuong qua doi.
Ngay 20 thang 4 nam 1729, Le Du Tong nhuong ngoi cho con la Duy Phuong.

Hon Duc Cong - Le Duy Phuong: Le Duy Phuong (1729-1732)
Thai tu Le Duy Phuong la con thu cua Le Du Tong, chau ngoai cua chua Trinh Cuong, duoc vua cha nhuong ngoi nam 1729, nhung khi Trinh Cuong mat thi ngoi vua cua Duy Phuong khong dung vung.

Le Y Tong - Le Duy Thin: Le Y Tong (1735-1740)
Le Y Tong ten huy la Duy Thin lem lam vua moi 17 tuoi.
Chua Trinh Giang la ke vo cung bao nguoc, sat hai cong than, giet vua no lap vua kia. Vi the, thang 12 nam 1738, cac ton that nha Le nhu Duy Mat, Duy Quy (con Du Tong), Duy Chuc (con Hy Tong) noi len chiem cu mien thuong du tay nam Thanh Hoa chong nhau voi nha Trinh rong ra 30 nam.
Tu ngay giet vua, Trinh Giang ngay cang lan at quyen vua. Trinh Giang tha ho an choi trac tang, vi the mac benh kinh quy, so sam set. Bon hoan quan Hoang Cong Phu cho dao dat lam cung thuong tri duoi ham cho Trinh Giang o. Bon hoan quan tha ho lung doan trieu dinh. Cac quan dai than ke tiep nhau bi giet hai, thue khoa nang ne, long dan ly tan. Cac cuoc khoi nghia cua nhan dan no ra khap noi.
Truoc tinh hinh nguy ngap do. Trinh Thai Phi (me Trinh Giang va Trinh Duong) cho trieu quan than den dua Trinh Doanh len thay Trinh Giang de tru hoan nan.
Nam 1740, Trinh Doanh len thay Trinh Giang, thang 5 nam 1740, Trinh Doanh ep Le y Tong nhuong ngoi cho con truong cua Thuan Tong la Duy Dieu. 19 nam sau Le y Tong mat, tho 40 tuoi, tri vi duoc 5 nam.

Next
Le Hien Tong - Le Duy Dieu: Le Hien Tong (1740-1786)
Le Hien Tong ten huy la Duy Dieu, la ong vua tri vi lau thu 2 trong lich su phong kien nuoc ta - 46 nam, va tho 70 tuoi.
Nho co tai giup do cua Minh Vuong Trinh Doanh nen muoi nam sau dat nuoc tro lai binh yen, dan duoc an cu lac nghiep, duoc ca ngoi la thoi thai binh.
Thang Gieng nam 1764, Hien Tong lap Duy Vi lam thai tu. Nam 1767 Trinh Doanh mat, Trinh Sam len thay lam nguyen soai Tinh Do Vuong, thang 3 nam 1769, Trinh Sam truat ngoi thai tu cua Duy Vi roi bat giam vao nguc. Thang 8 nam 1769, Trinh Sam gia menh vua truat thai tu lam thu dan roi van giam o nguc, lap Duy Can con thu cua Le Hien Tong lam thai tu.
Thang 12/1771, Trinh Sam sai giet thai tu Duy Vi, den thang Gieng nam 1783 lap Duy Ky lam Hoang Thai Ton, truat Duy Can lam Sung nhuong cong.

Next
Le Chieu Thong - Le Duy Ky: Le Man De (Chieu Thong, 1787-1788)
Le Chieu Thong ten huy la Duy Ky, chau dich ton cua Le Hien Tong.
Duy Ky duoc quan tam phu dua tu trai giam ve ep vua va chua Trinh Khai lap lam Hoang Thai Ton.Thang 7/1786, truoc khi vua Le Hien Tong mat da cho goi Thai Ton, Duy Ky vao troi loi truyen ngoi. Duy Ky vua lay vua khoc xin nhan menh. Luc do Bac Binh Vuong Nguyen Hue da cuoi cong chua Ngoc Han.
Khi Nguyen Nhac va Nguyen Hue rut quan ve Phu Xuan (Hue) thi cac hao muc o cac noi noi day cat cu. Trinh Bong cung tro lai kinh do Thang Long tu lap lam nguyen soai Yen Do vuong va lan at nha vua nhu truoc, khien cho trieu chinh roi ren, chem giet lan nhau. Nguyen Hue lai phai keo quan ra Bac dep loan, chieu tap cac cuu than van vo nha Le, lay Sung Nhuong Cong Le Duy Can dung lam Giam quoc va giao cho Ngo Van So lam Dai do doc o Thang Long, roi Nguyen Hue lai rut quan ve Phu Xuan.
De khoi phuc lai trieu Le, thang 7/1788 Hoang thai hau nha Le sang cau vien nha Man Thanh.
Dua vao the quan Man Thanh, Le Chieu Thong tro lai kinh do Thang Long da tra thu tan bao nhung nguoi theo Tay Son.
Mong 5 tet nam Ky Dau (1789), quan doi Tay Son do hoang de Quang Trung truc tiep chi huy keo ra Bac da danh tan 29 van quan xam luoc Man Thanh o go Dong Da gianh doc lap cho to quoc ta. Le Chieu Thong, vua ban nuoc, da cung 25 bay toi chay theo tan quan nha Thanh sang Trung Quoc.
Sau nam nam song luu vong nhuc nha tren dat Man Thanh, thang 10 nam Quy Suu (1793), Le Chieu Thong chet o Yen Kinh (Trung Quoc) tho 28 tuoi, o ngoi vua chua duoc 3 nam.
Nhu vay nha Le Trung Hung tu Le Trang Tong den vua Le Chieu Thong trai qua 16 doi vua voi 255 nam tri vi.

The To Minh Khang - Thai Vuong Trinh Kiem: The to Minh khang Thai vuong (Trinh Kiem, 1545-1570)
Trinh Kiem nguoi lang Soc Son, huyen Vinh Loc, tinh Thanh Hoa. Truyen ke rang: Trinh Kiem mo coi cha tu nho, nha ngheo hai me con rau chao nuoi nhau, khi me da gia thi Kiem di lam thue, ganh muon nuoi me. Mot hom di lam ve, khong thay me, Kiem bo di tim den sang thi duoc tin me anh da chet duoi o vuc gan nha, khi ra vuc, Kiem thay moi da dun thanh go roi, Kiem buon lam bo lang ra di, vao nuong nho lam gia tuong cho thai pho Nguyen Kim.
Trinh Kiem khong duoc hoc hanh nhieu, nhung rat thong minh, can dam va muu luoc hon nguoi. Nguyen Kim men tai dem con gai yeu la Ngoc Bao ga cho Kiem. Nam 1533, Nguyen Kim sai Trinh Kiem dem quan sang Ai Lao don Le Duy Ninh ve lap lam vua la Le Trang Tong. Vua thay Trinh Kiem tuong mao khac thuong, ben phong cho la Dai tuong quan, luc do Kiem 37 tuoi.
Nam At Ty - 1545, Nguyen Kim bi hang tuong nha Mac danh thuoc doc chet, binh quyen ve tay Trinh Kiem.
Vua Le Trang Tong o ngoi chi ton nhung quyen hanh deu do Trinh Kiem nam giu.
Nam 1548 Trang Tong mat, Trinh Kiem lap thai tu Huyen (con Trang Tong) len noi ngoi la Trung Tong. Vua Trung Tong cung chi o ngoi duoc 8 nam thi mat khi moi 22 tuoi, khong co con noi ngoi. Trinh Kiem muon nhan dip nay tu lap lam vua, nhung con phan van, ben sai Phung Khac Khoan di hoi y kien Trang Trinh. Cu Trang tra loi voi tham y khuyen Trinh Kiem hay ton pho nha Le cho thuan long dan. Hieu y, Trinh Kiem sai nguoi den lang Bo Ve ruoc Le Duy Bang ve lap lam vua tuc la Le Anh Tong.
Tu khi nam quyen binh, Trinh Kiem ra suc cung co luc luong, thu hut nhan tai, nen Nam trieu ngay cang manh len. Nha Mac (Bac trieu) sai dai tuong Mac Kinh Dien dem quan vao danh Thanh Hoa toi 10 lan, nguoc lai Trinh Kiem cung keo quan ra danh Son Nam truoc sau 6 lan. Nam trieu da lay lai duoc cac huyen Yen Mo, Yen Khang, Phung Hoa, Gia Vien. Nam Ky Ty - 1569, vua Le gia phong cho Trinh Kiem lam Thuong tuong Thai quoc cong va ton la Thuong phu.
Thang 2 nam Mau Ngo - 1570, Trinh Kiem mat, truy ton lam Minh khang Thai vuong, thuy la Trung Huan.
Trinh Kiem nam quyen cua Nam trieu 26 nam trai qua ba doi vua, tho 68 tuoi.

Next
Binh An Vuong - Trinh Tung: Binh An Vuong (Trinh Tung, 1570-1623)
Khi Trinh Kiem chet, vua Le Anh Tong trao quyen binh cho Trinh Coi (con vo ca cua Trinh Kiem). Nhung Trinh Coi ham me tuu sac, ngay cang kieu ngao, can ro, cac tuong linh khong phuc.
Thang 4 nam Canh Ngo - 1570, cac tuong nhu Le Cap De, Trinh Vinh Thieu, Trinh Bach, Phan Cong Tich dem quan theo ve voi Trinh Tung. Trinh Tung la con thu cua Trinh Kiem voi Ngoc Bao (con gai Nguyen Kim). Tung khoi ngo tuan tu, co tai thao luoc, trong nhan tai nen duoc tuong si yeu men.
Trinh Tung cung cac tuong si pho gia vua Le Anh Tong vao thanh Van Lai, chia quan canh giu de phong Trinh Coi. Trinh Coi dich than dem hon mot van quan den bao vay thanh Van Lai.
Hai ben danh nhau giang co bay ngay, vua Le Anh Tong dung ra hoa giai cung khong duoc. Cuoi cung Trinh Coi phai rut quan ve Bien Thuong.
Duoc tin anh em ho Trinh danh nhau, thang 8 nam Canh Ngo - 1570, vua Mac sai Mac Kinh Dien dem 10 van quan va 700 chien thuyen vao danh Thanh Hoa. Trinh Coi lo so, voi dem me, vo con va cac thuoc tuong den hang nha Mac. Mac Kinh Dien phong cho Coi lam Trung Luong hau.
Vua Le Anh Tong phong cho Trinh Tung lam Truong quan cong, Tiet che thuy bo chu dinh cam quan danh Mac.
Thang 12 nam do, sau 4 thang tan cong vao Thanh Hoa khong thang duoc. Mac Kinh Dien phai rut quan ve Bac. Trinh Coi cung me va vo con chay theo quan Mac.
Nam Nham Than - 1572, Le Cap De muu giet Trinh Tung bi lo, Le Cap De bi Trinh Tung giet chet. Vua Le Anh Tong so hai dang dem dem 4 hoang tu chay vao thanh Nghe An.
Trinh Tung dua hoang tu thu 5 cua vua Le la Duy Dam len lam vua, hieu la Le The Tong.
Sau hon muoi nam lien tuc mo cac cuoc tan cong ra Bac, cuoi cung Trinh Tung da danh bai duoc nha Mac. Khoi phuc duoc co do Thang Long vao nam 1592.
Nam At Mui - 1595, Trinh Tung don vua vao Thang Long va bat dau to chuc bo may cai tri theo quy mo cua bac de vuong. Trinh Tung sai su sang nha Minh xin sac phong cho vua Le la An Nam thong su, va buoc vua Le phong cho minh lam Do nguyen suy Tong quan quoc chinh thuong phu, tuoc Binh An vuong.
Trinh Tung cho lap phu lieu rieng gom du ca luc phien tuong duong voi luc bo cua trieu vua. Phu chua toan quyen dat quan chuc, thu thue, bat linh... Vua chi co mat trong nhung dip long trong dac biet nhu tiep su Tau ma thoi. Tu day bat dau thoi ky "vua Le - chua Trinh". Con chua Trinh duoc quyen the tap goi la The tu.
Truoc su hong hach long quyen cua chua Trinh, vua Le Kinh Tong da cung voi con Trinh Tung la Trinh Xuan muu giet Trinh Tung. Viec bai lo, Trinh Tung buc vua that co chet, luc do moi 32 tuoi. Tung dua thai tu Duy Ky len ngoi vua la Le Than Tong.
Ngay 20 thang 6 nam Quy Hoi - 1623, Trinh Tung mat, cam quyen 53 nam, tho 74 tuoi.

Next
Thanh Do Vuong - Trinh Trang: Thanh Do Vuong (Trinh Trang, 1623-1652)
Mac Kinh Khoan dang chiem cu Cao Bang nghe tin Trinh Tung chet, cac con (Trinh Xuan, Trinh Trang) danh nhau de gianh ngoi chua, lien tu Cao Bang keo hang van quan xuong Gia Lam. Trinh Trang phai ruoc vua Le chay vao Thanh Hoa.
Thang 8 nam Qui Hoi - 1523, Trinh Trang dem quan ra pha tan quan cua Kinh Khoan. Kinh Khoan mot minh chay thoat than. Trinh Trang lai ruoc vua Le tro lai kinh do, vua Le phong cho Trinh Trang chuc Nguyen suy thong quoc chinh Thanh Do Vuong.
Tam yen mat Bac, Trinh Trang lo doi pho mat Nam. Luc nay o Dang Trong, chua Nguyen Phuc Nguyen da ra mat chong lai chua Trinh o Dang Ngoai. Trinh Trang nhieu lan lay danh nghia vua Le dem quan di danh Phuc Nguyen nhung quan Nguyen loi dung dia the hiem tro, dap luy dai chong cu quyet liet, quan Trinh khong lam gi duoc, phai rut ve.
De that chat them quan he gan bo giua nha chua va vua Le, Trinh Trang dem con gai cua minh (da lay chong co bon con voi chu ho cua vua Le) ga cho vua Le, ep vua lap lam Hoang hau. Vua Le phai chap nhan.
Nam at Dau - 1645, Trinh Trang xin vua Le phong cho con thu hai la Tay quan cong Trinh Tac lam Kham sai tiet che cac su thuy bo chu dinh chuong quoc quyen binh, ta tuong thai uy Tay quoc cong va duoc quyen noi ngoi chua.
Nam Dinh Dau - 1657, Trinh Trang mat tho 81 tuoi, o ngoi chua 30 nam.

Tay Dinh Vuong - Trinh Tac: Tay Do Vuong (Trinh Tac, 1653-1682)
Trinh Tac la con thu hai duoc cha chon lam Nguyen suy chuong quoc chinh Tay Dinh Vuong tu nam Qui Ty - 1653, khi Trinh Trang dang con song. Su khong chon Trinh Toan la con truong noi ngoi chua da dan den mau thuan ngam ngam giua Toan va Tac.
Nam Dinh Dau - 1657, Trinh Tac da sai dinh than tong nguc va tra hoi Trinh Toan cho den chet.
Nam Dinh Mui - 1667, Trinh Tac tu gia phong Dai nguyen soai thuong su Thai phu Tay Do Vuong.
Nam Nham Ti - 1672, Trinh Tac ep vua Le dem quan vao danh chua Nguyen mot tran lon o chau Bo Chinh, quan Trinh chiem duoc luy Tran Ninh, huy dong 3 van quan vuot song Gianh danh vao cua Nhat Le. Quan Nguyen ra suc chong cu nho co he thong thanh luy kien co, thang 12/1672, Trinh Tac phai rut dai binh ve chi de Le Thoi Hien tran giu Nghe An. Tu do Dang Ngoai va Dang Trong tam ngung chien, lay song Gianh lam gioi tuyen.
Thang 7 nam Giap Dan - 1674, Trinh Tac xin vua Le phong cho con la Trinh Can lam Nguyen soai. Nam Tan Dau - 1682, Trinh Tac mat, tho 77 tuoi, o ngoi chua 29 nam.

Dinh Nam Vuong - Trinh Can: Dinh Vuong (Trinh Can, 1682-1709)
Trinh Can la con truong cua Trinh Tac duoc noi ngoi chua la Dinh Vuong.
Duoi thoi Trinh Can, chien tranh Trinh - Nguyen da tam dung. Trinh Can co dieu kien de cung co bo may cai tri. Giup viec cho chua Trinh luc do co nhieu nguoi do dat cao nhu Nguyen Danh Nho, Nguyen Tong Quai, Nguyen Qui Duc, Dang Dinh Tuong nen da on dinh duoc xa hoi, kinh te phat trien.
Bang nhieu co gang ngoai giao, chua Trinh buoc nha Thanh tra lai mot so thon ap vung bien gioi ma ho da lan chiem cua ta, nhung chua duoc nhieu.
Chua Trinh Can gap nhieu trac tro trong viec lap nguoi ke nghiep. Nam Giap Ti - 1684, Trinh Can phong cho con thu la Trinh Bach lam Tiet che thay cho con ca la Vinh da chet, nhung den nam Dinh Mao - 1687, Trinh Bach lai chet som. Trinh Can lai phong cho Trinh Binh la chau noi dich ton (con Trinh Vinh) noi ngoi, nhung den nam Qui Mui - 1703, Trinh Binh cung chet, Trinh Can lai phai phong cho chat noi (con ca Trinh Binh) la Trinh Cuong lam Tiet che An quoc cong.
Nam Ky Suu - 1709, Trinh Can mat. Chat la Trinh Cuong len noi ngoi. Trinh Can tho 77 tuoi o ngoi chua 27 nam.

An Do Vuong - Trinh Cuong: An do vuong (Trinh Cuong, 1709-1729)
Trinh Cuong len ngoi chua, duoc phong lam Nguyen soai tong quoc chinh An do vuong nam 1709.
Thang 9 nam Giap Ngo (17 14) Trinh Cuong lai duoc tien phong Dai nguyen soai tong quoc chinh Thuong su An do vuong.
Trinh Cuong biet giu moi quan he tot voi vua Le, dong thoi cham lo viec tri nuoc. Chua trong dung ba dai than rat tre nhung co tai la Le Anh Tuan, Nguyen Cong Co va Nguyen Cong Hang. Ho da dua ra chu truong cai cach tren nhieu linh vuc ve kinh te, tai chinh, thi cu, to chuc hanh chinh.. . nhung cac cai cach tien bo do dang duoc tien hanh thi Trinh Cuong mat.
Nam Ky Dau (1729), Trinh Cuong di vang canh chua Phat Tich roi Nhu Kinh, bi benh, chet ngay tai do, quan quan bi mat dua ve phu Chua phat tang. Tiec thay vi chua co nhieu tam huyet voi cong cuoc cai cach kinh te, chinh tri da mat o tuoi 44, o ngoi chua 20 nam.

Uy Nam Vuong - Trinh Giang: Uy nam vuong (Trinh Giang, 1729-1740)
Trinh Giang la con ca Trinh Cuong. Khi Giang con lam The tu, bao pho cua Giang la Nguyen Cong Hang da dang mot so nhan xet rang Giang la nguoi uon hen khong the ganh vac duoc ngoi chua. Trinh Cuong da co y thay ngoi The tu, nhung chua dut khoat thi Trinh Cuong dot ngot mat, Trinh Giang voi tu cach la The tu len noi ngoi chua.
Thang 4 nam Canh Tuat (1739) Giang tu tien phong la Nguyen soai thong quoc chinh Uy nam vuong.
Chua Trinh Giang la ke vo cung bao nguoc, sat hai cong than, giet vua Le Duy Phuong, lap vua Le y Tong. Trinh Giang ngay cang lan at quyen vua, tha ho an choi trac tang. Mot hom Trinh Giang bi set danh gan chet nen mac benh kinh hai, so sam set. Bon hoan quan Hoang Cong Phu cho dao dat lam Cung Thuong Tri duoi ham cho Trinh Giang o. Bon hoan quan tha ho lung doan trieu chinh. Cac quan dai than ke tiep nhau bi giet hai, thue khoa nang ne, long dan ly tan. Cac cuoc khoi nghia cua nhan dan no ra khap noi. Truoc tinh hinh nguy ngap do, Trinh Thai Phi (me Trinh Giang va Trinh Doanh) cho trieu quan than den dua Trinh Doanh len thay Trinh Giang de tru hoa nan.
Trinh Giang o ngoi chua 11 nam, nam 1760 moi mat tho 51 tuoi.

Minh Do Vuong - Trinh Doanh: Minh do vuong (Trinh Doanh, 1740-1767)
Nam Canh Than (1740), 'Trinh Doanh len ngoi chua lay hieu la Minh do vuong tien ton Trinh Giang lam Thai thuong vuong.
Trinh Doanh lien ban hanh nhieu quyet dinh hop long nguoi, duoc quan than va dan chung ung ho. Chinh sach cai tri duoi thoi Trinh Doanh kha chac chan va hoan chinh. Nhieu sac chi duoc ban hanh duoi thoi Trinh Cuong (da bi Trinh Giang bo) nay duoc thuc hien.
Trinh Doanh cham lo chinh su. Cho dat ong dong o cua phu de nhan thu tu dan chung to giac viec lam sai trai cua quan lai: Khi can tuyen chon va cat nhac quan lai, Trinh Doanh coi trong thuc tai nen truoc khi bo nhiem ai nguoi do phai vao phu duong yet kien de chua truc tiep hoi ve viec la lam. ai co kha nang moi trao cho chuc quyen. Chua thuong phat rat cong minh. Nhieu danh si xuat than khoa bang duoc trong dung, tieu bieu la Le Qui Don. Ngo Thi Si...
Lich su da ghi nhan nhung nam Trinh Doanh cam quyen o Bac Ha la thoi ky dat nuoc on dinh va thinh dat.
Song khi moi len ngoi chua, de dep loan bang moi gia Trinh Doanh da mac mot sai lam khong the tha thu la dot het so sach thu tu, thu nhat het chuong khanh cac chua chien de duc binh khi.
Thang Gieng nam Dinh Hoi (1767). Trinh Doanh mat tho 48 tuoi, o ngoi chua 27 nam.

Tinh Do Vuong - Trinh Sam: Tinh do vuong (Trinh Sam, 1767-1782)
Trinh Sam la con truong cua Trinh Doanh. Nam at Suu (1745) Sam duoc lap lam The tu. Trinh Doanh cu hai tien si danh tieng la Duong Cong Chu va Nguyen Hoan lam tu giang cho Trinh Sam.
Nam Dinh Hoi (1767). Trinh Doanh mat, Trinh Sam len ngoi chua, tien phong la Nguyen soai Tong quoc chinh Tinh do vuong...
Trinh Sam la nguoi cung ran, thong minh, quyet doan. Khi len ngoi chua, Trinh Sam cho sua doi ky cuong, chinh su ca nuoc vi cho rang phep tac cua trieu truoc la nho hep, nay Sam muon lam to rong hon, nen phan nhieu tu quyet doan, khong theo phep cu.
Nam Ky Suu (1769), sau hai nam len ngoi vi ghen ghet tai nang, duc do va dia vi cua Thai tu Duy Vi, Sam da vu toi cho Thai tu, sai nguoi bat giu, truat ngoi va tong giam, chet trong nguc.
Nam Canh Mao (1770), sau khi danh tan Le Duy Mat, buoc Duy Mat tu tu, Trinh Sam kieu man, cho minh co cong lon nen tu tien phong la Dai nguyen soai tong quoc chinh, Thuong su thuong phu, Due doan van cong vo duc Tinh do vuong.
Nam Giap Ngo (1774), de khuech truong thanh the, Sam sai Hoang Ngu Phuc lam thong tuong dem quan vao Nam danh chua Nguyen, thang 10 nam do, Sam dich than cam quan keo vao Thuan Hoa, chiem duoc Thuan Hoa va dat quan cai tri dat Thuan Quang, trong do co Le Quy Don, tac gia sach ''Phu bien tap luc".
Trinh Sam sau khi da dep yen cac cuoc khoi nghia, bon phuong yen on, kho dun day du, dan dan sinh bung kieu cang: xa xi, ken nhieu than phi thi nu, mac y vui choi thoa thich.
Dang Thi Hue la nu ty cua tiep du Tran Thi Vinh, a mat phuong may ngai, ve nguoi rat xinh dep va hap dan. Trinh Sam trong thay dem long yeu men dac biet.
Dang Thi Hue duoc song voi chua nhu vo chong. Xe hieu quan ao cua a deu duoc sam sua nhu cua chua.
De chieu long nguoi dep, moi nam cu den tet Trung Thu la cho to chuc dem ''Hoi Long Tri" treo hang ngan chiec den long, trong xa tua hang van ngoi sao sang de vui choi thoa thich.
Chua Trinh Sam con ga con gai yeu cho em trai Thi Hue la Dang Mau Lan, mot ten ,du con y quyen the chuyen cuop boc, cuong hiep phu nu giua pho phuong.
Dang Thi Hue con lien ket voi Huy quan cong Hoang Dinh Bao gat bo The tu Trinh Khai (con ca cua Trinh Sam) lap Trinh Can (con cua Thi Hue) lam the tu moi 5 tuoi.
Thang 9 nam Nham Dan (1782), Trinh Sam mat, tho 44 tuoi, o ngoi chua 15 nam.

Dien Do Vuong - Trinh Can: Dien Do Vuong (Trinh Can, 2 thang trong nam 1782)
Trinh Sam mat, Dang Thi Hue va Huy quan cong Hoang Dinh Bao lap Trinh Can len ngoi chua voi tuoc hieu Dien do vuong, luc do Can moi 6 tuoi. Tuyen phi Dang Thi Hue nghiem nhien tro thanh nguoi dieu khien trieu chinh giup con. Long nguoi lo so, tu phu chua ra kinh thanh, thon da, ai cung biet chac hoa loan sap xay ra.
Thang 10 nam Nham Dan - 1782, Du Vu la tay chan cua Trinh Khai xui kieu binh (linh Tam phu) noi loan, truat ngoi Can, giang xuong lam Cung quoc cong, giet chet Hoang Dinh Bao. Dang Thi Hue bi truat xuong thu dan, sau tu tu. Trinh Can bi dua ra o phu Luong quoc, om chet, o ngoi duoc gan hai thang.

Doan Nam Vuong - Trinh Khai: Doan Nam Vuong (Trinh Khai, 1782-1786)
Linh Tam phu noi loan lat do Trinh Can dua Trinh Khai len ngoi chua, tien phong la Doan nam vuong.
Thang 6 nam Binh Ngo - 1786, dang luc phu chua roi ren, khon kho vi nan kieu binh hoanh hanh khap kinh ky, dan chung ngay dem nom nop lo so thi nghia quan Tay Son cua Nguyen Hue voi danh nghia "phu Le diet Trinh" keo ra Bac Ha.
Quan Trinh chong cu yeu ot, mau chong tan ra, bo chay.
Tuong Hoang Phung Co, Nguyen Le rut chay len phia Bac, Trinh Khai mac nhung phuc, cuoi voi, cam co lenh chi huy, nhung quan si da bo chay het. Trinh Khai phai mot minh bo chay len Son Tay.
Trinh Khai bi Nguyen Trang bat giai ve nop cho quan Tay Son, tren duong giai ve Trinh Khai dung dao tu tu.
Trinh Khai lam chua chua duoc 4 nam thi chet, luc do moi 24 tuoi. 12. an do vuong (Trinh Bong, 1787-1788)
Trinh Bong la con Trinh Giang, bac ruot cua Trinh Khai. Trinh Bong da duoc phong la Con quan cong. Khi Trinh Khai bo tron len Son Tay, Trinh Bong lanh nan o huyen Van Giang (Hung Yen).
Khi Nguyen Hue va vua Thai Duc Nguyen Nhac rut ve Nam Ha, Trinh Bong da ve yet kien vua Le, duoc vua Le phong cho lam Nguyen soai, Tong quoc chinh, an do vuong.
Trinh Bong nhu nhuoc, luoi bieng, khong dieu khien cong viec, do do chinh su lai vao tay Dinh Tich Nhuong, chung lan at vua Le, tu do vua va chua lai cang mau thuan. Vua Le da voi Nguyen Huu Chinh tu Nghe An dem quan ve giup.
Trinh Bong tron chay ve xa Duong Xa roi Que Vo (Bac Ninh). Trinh Bong di Hai Duong - Quang Yen va Thai Binh mo quan luong muu danh lai Nguyen Huu Chinh, nhung danh may tran deu that bai.
Nam 1788, Trinh Bong sau nhieu lan that bai, da bo di tu o vung Lang Son, Cao Bang.

An Do Vuong - Trinh Bong: An Do Vuong (Trinh Bong) (1787-1788)
Trinh Bong la con Trinh Giang, bac ruot cua Trinh Khai. Trinh Bong da duoc phong la Con quan cong. Khi Trinh Khai bo tron len Son Tay, Trinh Bong lanh nan o huyen Van Giang (Hung Yen).

Tien Vuong - Nguyen Hoang: Nguyen Hoang (Chua Tien, 1558-1613)
Nguyen Kim que o Gia Mieu, Tong Son, Ha Trung, Thanh Hoa, la con cua Trung quoc cong Nguyen Hoang Du da tung giup vua Le Tuong Duc khoi binh o Thanh Hoa lat do Le Uy Muc, nhan do duoc phong la Thai pho Trung quoc cong.
Mac Dang Dung cuop ngoi nha Le, Nguyen Kim lanh sang Ai Lao, thu nap hao kiet, pho giup nha Le trung hung, duoc vua Le Trang Tong phong la Thuong phu Thai su Hung quoc cong. Nam Canh Ty - l540 Nguyen Kim dem quan ve chiem Nghe An, nam l542 chiem duoc Tay Do - Thanh Hoa. Nam at Ti - 1545, Nguyen Kim bi hang tuong nha Mac la Duong Chap Nhat dau doc chet, tho 75 tuoi, quyen hanh roi vao tay con re la Trinh Kiem.
Khi Nguyen Kim chay sang Ai Lao thi Nguyen Hoang moi 2 tuoi, duoc Thai pho Nguyen U Di la cau ruot nuoi day nen nguoi.
Trinh Kiem la anh re, muon thau tom quyen hanh nen da ngam ngam am hai cac em vo. Nguyen Uong, con truong cua Nguyen Kim da bi ham hai. Nguyen Hoang rat lo so, sai nguoi den yet kien Trang Trinh Nguyen Binh Khiem xin mach cho ke an toan, Trang Trinh da ung khau cau tho: ''Hoanh Son nhat dai, van dai dung than!'' (mot day nui Hoanh Son co the dung than muon doi).
Nguyen Hoang nghi ra, den noi voi chi gai la Ngoc Bao xin voi anh re la Trinh Kiem cho vao tran thu Thuan Hoa la noi hoang vu nhieu giac da. Trinh Kiem dong y cho Nguyen Hoang vao tran thu Thuan Hoa voi y do muon tay giac giet em vo.
Nam Mau Ngo - 1558, Trinh Kiem tau voi vua Le Anh Tong cho Nguyen Hoang vao tran thu Thuan Hoa.
Duoc lenh vao Nam, bat chap mua dong gia ret, Nguyen Hoang giong buom di ngay, nhung nguoi ho hang o huyen Tong Son cung quan linh o dat Thanh - Nghe nhieu nguoi dem ca vo con di theo co den nghin nguoi. Cac danh than cung di co Nguyen U Di, Tong Phuoc Tri, Mac Canh Huong...
Vao Nam, doan nguoi cua Nguyen Hoang do bo len cang Cua Viet, dung dinh thu o ai Tu thuoc huyen Dang Xuong, Quang Tri.
Nguyen Hoang la nguoi khon ngoan, nhan hau, luu tam den dan tinh, het long thu dung hao kiet. ong giam suu, ha thue khien long nguoi ai cung men phuc. Nhan dan xung tung ong la chua Tien.
De tranh su nghi ky cua chua Trinh, nam 1569, ong ra chau vua Le o An Truong, duoc vua Le, chua Trinh khen ngoi. Nam 1570, Nguyen Hoang duoc giao tran thu luon dat Quang Nam voi an Tong tran. ong doi do vao lang Tra Bat (tuc Cat Dinh) cung thuoc huyen Dang Xuong.
Nam 1572, tuong Lap Bao cua nha Mac theo duong bien dem quan vao dong o hai lang Ho Xa va Lang Uyen (Quang Tri) dinh pha su nghiep o Thuan - Quang cua Nguyen Hoang. Nguyen Hoang da dung ke my nhan pha duoc am muu cua Lap Bao va danh tan duoc quan nha Mac.
Nam 1593, Nguyen Hoang dem quan ra Dong Do giup Trinh Tung danh dep du dang ho Mac. Vi lap duoc nhieu chien cong, ong duoc vua Le tan phong lam Trung quan Do doc phu ta do doc chuong phu su thai uy Doan quoc cong. De tranh Trinh Tung ham hai, nam Canh Ty - 1600 Nguyen Hoang lay co dem quan di dep cuoc noi loan o Nam Dinh, sau do cung cac tuong tam phuc ra bien giong thang vao dat Thuan - Quang de con trai thu 5 la Hai va chau la Hac o lai lam con tin. Vua Le sai su gia vao phu du va van de Nguyen Hoang tran thu Thuan - Quang. Thang 10 nam Canh Ty - 1600, Nguyen Hoang ga con gai la Ngoc Tu cho Trinh Trang (con ca Trinh Tung).
Co the noi tu nam 1600, Nguyen Hoang bat dau xay dung giang son rieng cho ho Nguyen o Dang Trong.
Thuan - Quang von la dat cu cua nguoi Cham chiu anh huong van hoa Champa, chua Tien da dung Phat giao de thuan hoa nhan dan. ong sua sang va xay dung nhieu ngoi chua.
Dac biet, nam 1601 ong cho xay dung chua Thien Mu la cong viec to lon co gia tri nhat. Ngoi chua lich su nay da co quan he mat thiet voi qua trinh phat trien dat Thuan Hoa va trieu Nguyen o Viet Nam.
Nguyen Hoang sinh ngay 28/8/1525, mat ngay 21/5/1613 tho 89 tuoi, tran thu Thuan - Quang 55 nam (1558-1613). ong sinh 10 con trai va 2 con gai. Sau nay, trieu Nguyen truy ton ong la Thai to Gia du Hoang de.

Sai vuong - Nguyen Phuc Nguyen: Nguyen Phuc Nguyen (Chua Sai, 1613-1635) Nguyen Hoang sap mat, goi con thu 6 la Nguyen Phuc Nguyen vao dan do: Dat Thuan - Quang nay phia bac thi co nui Hoanh Son, song Linh Giang, phia nam thi co nui Hai Van va nui Bi Son that la noi troi de cho nguoi anh hung dung vo. Thuong vuong - Nguyen Phuc Lan: Chua Nguyen Phuc Lan (chua Thuong, 1635-1648)

Nghe tin Phuc Lan noi nghiep, hoang tu thu 3 la Nguyen Phuc Anh dang tran giu Quang Nam am muu phan nghich, lien ket voi chua Trinh dem quan vao danh mien Nam. Phuc Anh sai dap luy Cau Ðe lam ke co thu. Phuc Lan danh bat duoc, khong no giet nguoi ruot thit, nhung tuong si va ong chu la Truong quan cong Nguyen Phuc Khe deu xin giet de tru hau hoa.
Chua du dau xot cung phai nghe theo.
Nam Ky Mao (1639), Tong Thi (vo goa cua Phuc Anh) dang cho chua chuoi hoa vong ngoc lien chau rat dep. Chua cam len ngui thay mui huong thom ngat xuc dong long yeu. Tong Thi lai vao sup lay duoi them, thua trinh ve tinh canh goa bua tham thiet, nhan sac lai cuc ky diem le. Chua Thuong noi tinh rieng, sau do moi nang vao noi that chung chan goi.
Tu do chua rat muc sung ai Tong Thi. Nang trinh bam viec gi chua cung nghe theo. Tong Thi lua loi kheo leo de chua vui long, nghi cach chiem doat cua cai cua dan de lam giau rieng. Cac quan dai than cam ghet Tong Thi, tim cach can gian nhung chua khong nghe. Cho hay, nhan sac, gai dep co manh luc vo bien, lam lung lac ca dang quan vuong.
Lam chua duoc 9 nam, vi chua da tinh nay lap duoc chien cong vang doi. Lan dau tien trong lich su, thuy quan Viet Nam da danh thang thuy quan Au Chau.
Ðo la nam 1643, Ha Lan theo yeu cau cua chua Trinh da cho 3 chiec tau dong kieu tron, trang bi nhieu trong phao tien vao cua Eo (Thuan An) muu do xam luoc nuoc ta.
Chua Thuong hop quan than ban dinh co nen dua chien thuyen cua minh ra danh tau Ha Lan hay khong. Cac quan than khong dam hua la chac thang. Chua hoi mot nguoi Ha Lan dang giup viec cho chua. Nguoi ay tu phu tra loi: Tau Ha Lan chi so manh luc va quan doi cua troi thoi. Nghe vay, chua cam thay bi xuc pham. Ong than hanh den Eo, ra lenh cho thuy quan cheo thuyen ra danh thang vao 3 chiec tau cua Ha Lan.
Hang tram chiec thuyen Viet Nam xong thang vao cac chiec tau Ha Lan, mac dai bac ban ra nhu mua. Bon mat tau Ha Lan deu bi tan cong. Nho thuyen nho co dong, nhanh nhen nen mac du bi mot so dan, thuyen Viet Nam van bao vay tan cong vao tau Ha Lan quyet liet. Chung vo cung kinh hoang, khong ngo thuy quan chua Nguyen lai gan da den the. Chiec nho nhat voi luon lach chay thoat than. Chiec thu hai thang thot dam vao da, ca doan thuy thu va tau chim nghim xuong bien. Chiec thu 3 lon nhat chong cu lai, cac thuy quan chua Nguyen bam sat tau be banh lai. Mot so nhay len tau, chat gay cot buom. Bi don vao the tuyet vong, thuyen truong Ha Lan cho no kho thuoc sung. The la tat ca thuy thu bi hoa thieu chet la liet tren bien. Co 7 ten tren tau nhay xuong bien, co boi thoat nhung deu bi tom co.
Thang tran tro ve, chua Thuong dan 7 ten tu binh den truoc mat nguoi Ha Lan noi:
- Can chi manh luc va quan doi cua troi moi pha duoc. Chien thuyen cua ta cung kha day chu.
Nam 1648, Trinh Trang cho cac dao quan thuy bo danh vao mien Nam. Bo binh dong o dat Nam Bo Chinh, con thuy quan thi danh vao cua Nhat Le. Nguyen Phuc Lan phai tu cam quan danh lai. Sau Phuc Lan thay trong nguoi khong duoc khoe, moi trao binh quyen cho con trai la Nguyen Phuc Tan va tuong Nguyen Huu Dat chi huy con minh thi rut ve. Ðen pha Tam Giang thi chua mat, tho den 48 tuoi, o ngoi chua 13 nam. Sau trieu Nguyen truy ton la Than ton Hien chieu Hoang de. Chua Thuong co 4 nguoi con (3 con trai, 1 con gai).

Hien vuong - Nguyen Phuc Tan: Nguyen Phuc Tan (chua Hien, 1648-1687)
Nguyen Phuc Tan sinh nam Canh Thin (1620). Luc dau duoc phong Pho tuong Dung le hau, da tung danh giac o cua bien, duoc chua Phuc Lan rat khen ngoi. Nam Mau Ty - 1648 duoc tan phong la tiet che chu quan, thay Phuc Lan pha quan Trinh o song Gianh. Bay gio ong 29 tuoi. Chua Nguyen Phuc Lan mat dot ngot, bay toi ton Nguyen Phuc Tan len ngoi chua, goi la chua Hien. Chua Nguyen Phuc Tan la nguoi cham chi chinh su, khong chuong yen tiec vui choi.
Phuc Tan biet tan dung hai tuong tai gioi la Nguyen Huu Dat va Nguyen Huu Tien. Quan chua Nguyen nhieu lan vuot song Gianh tien ra dat Dang Ngoai. Nam 1656, sau hai nam tan cong ta Bac, quan Nguyen da chiem duoc 7 huyen cua Nghe An. Tu than Nguyen Phuc Tan da dem quan ra den Nghe An dong tai xa Van Cat, quan Nguyen co the tien sau them nua, nhung nghe tin Trinh Trang mat, con la Trinh Tac len ngoi chua dang chiu tang, chua Nguyen cho nguoi sang dieu roi rut quan ve, luu cac tuong dong don tu song Lam tro ve Nam, dap luy tu nui den cua bien de phong ngu. Sau dot tan cong do, quan Nguyen con chiem dat Nghe An them 5 lan nua, nam 1660 chua Trinh moi khoi phuc lai duoc. Tu do Trinh - Nguyen cam cu nhau suot may chuc nam khong phan thang bai.
Nam Ky Mui - 1679, chua Nguyen cho phep Duong Ngan Dich, mot tuong cu cua trieu Minh cung voi Tran Thuong Xuyen dem gia thuoc hon 3000 nguoi va hon 50 chien thuyen den khai pha vung dat Gia Dinh - My Tho. Tu do pho xa, cho bua moc len sam uat, thuyen buon cua cac nuoc Thanh, Nhat Ban va cac nuoc phuong Tay ra vao tap nap, do do phong hoa ngay cang mo mang
Duoi thoi chua Hien, nhieu kenh dan nuoc tuoi ruong duoc khoi dao, nhu Trung Dan, Mai Xa. Bay gio bo coi duoc thai binh, thoc lua duoc mua. Chua cang cham lo chinh su, khong xay den dai, khong gan gai dep, bot lao dich thue khoa, nhan dan deu khen ngoi thoi thai binh thinh tri.
Nam Dinh Mao - 1687, chua Hien mat, tho 65 tuoi, o ngoi chua 39 nam. Trieu Nguyen truy ton ong la Thai tong hien triet hoang de. Chua Hien co 9 nguoi con (6 con trai, 3 con gai).

Nghia vuong - Nguyen Phuc Tran: Nguyen Phuc Thai (chua Nghia, 1687-1691)
Nguyen Phuc Tan co 6 nguoi con trai, Nguyen Phuc Thai la con thu 2 cua ba vo thu hai nguoi ho Tong, nhung lon tuoi va hien duc. Khi Nguyen Phuc Tan mat, Thai da 39 tuoi.
Nguyen Phuc Thai noi tieng la nguoi rong rai, hinh phat va phu thue da nhe, tram ho ai cung vui mung. Quan lai cu cua trieu truoc deu duoc trong dai.
Nguoi doi sau nhac den chua Nghia Nguyen Phuc Thai la nho chua da doi phu tu Kim Long ve Phu Xuan dia the bang phang, dep de, tiep noi nhieu doi chon lam kinh do.
Chua khong tho duoc lau. Sau 4 nam o ngoi chua, nam Tan Mui - 1691, Nguyen Phuc Thai mat, tho 43 tuoi. Trieu Nguyen truy ton ong la Anh tong hieu nghia hoang de. Chua Nghia co 10 nguoi con (5 con trai, 5 con gai).

Minh vuong - Nguyen Phuc Chu: Nguyen Phuc Chu la con ca Nguyen Phuc Thai, sinh nam at Mao - 1675, duoc an hoc kha can than vi the van hay chu tot, du tai luoc van vo. Khi noi ngoi Chua moi 17 tuoi. Chua Nguyen Phuc Chu mo dao Phat. Nam 1710 chua sai duc chuong lon nang toi 3.285 can, dat o chua Thien Mu va xay dung mot loat chua mieu khac. Chua cho mo hoi lon o chua Thien Mu, chua nui My An. Tu chua cung an chay o vuon Con Gia mot thang troi. Chua phat tien gao cho nguoi ngheo. Day la thoi ky ma chien tranh Trinh - Nguyen da tam ngung hon 30 nam, dat nuoc binh yen, chua Nguyen Phuc Chu co dieu kien mo mang dat dai ve phia Nam va dat duoc nhung thanh tuu dang ke: dat phu Binh Thuan nam Dinh Suu - 1697 gom dat Phan Rang, Phan Ri, chia lam hai huyen An Phuc va Hoa Da; bat dau dat phu Gia Dinh, chia dat Dong Pho lay xu Dong Nai lam huyen Phuc Long; dung dinh Tran Bien (tuc Bien Hoa) lay xu Sai Gon lam huyen Tan Binh; dung dinh Phien Tran; lap xa Minh Huong... Tu do nguoi Thanh di lai buon ban rat sam uat. Nam 1702, Cong ty an Do cua Anh do Allen Catohpole dem 200 quan den chiem dao Con Lon, chua Quoc lap tuc sai con la Nguyen Phuc Phan dung muu danh duoi ra khoi dao. Nam 1708, chua Quoc dung Mac Cuu lam Tong binh tran Ha Tien. Nam 1709, chua Quoc cho duc an Quoc Buu "Dai Viet Quoc, Nguyen Phuc Vinh Tran chi buu" an ay ve sau tro thanh vat bau truyen ngoi. Nam 1714, chua Quoc dai trung tu chua Thien Mu va di tham pho Hoi An. Nhan thay cau do nguoi Nhat lam tu tap nhieu thuyen buon cac nuoc, chua ben dat la Lai Vien Kieu va ban bien chu vang ngay nay van con bien do. Ngay 1/6/1725, chua Quoc mat, tho 51 tuoi, o ngoi 34 nam. Sau trieu Nguyen truy ton la Hien Tong Hieu Minh Hoang de. Chua Quoc co 42 nguoi con (38 con trai va 4 con gai).

Ninh vuong - Nguyen Phuc Thu: Nguyen Phuc Thu (Ninh Vuong, 1725-1738)
Nguyen Phuc Thu (nhieu sach viet la Chu) sinh ngay 14/1/1697, la con trai ca cua chua Quoc, khi chua Quoc mat duoc len ngoi Chua luc do da 30 tuoi, xung hieu la Ninh Vuong. Nam Quy Suu - 1733, chua cho dat dong ho mua cua Tay phuong o cac dinh va cac don tau doc bien. Sau co nguoi tho thu cong la Nguyen Van Tu che tao duoc chiec dong ho y het. Nam Binh Thin - 1736, Mac Cuu mat, con trai la Mac Thien Tu duoc chua cho lam Do doc tran Ha Tien. Mac Thien Tu la mot nha cai tri gioi, ma lai van tho hay, Mac Thien Tu mo Chieu Anh Cac de tu hop cac van nhan thi si cung nhau xuong hoa. Mac Thien Tu de lai 10 bai tho ca ngoi phong canh dep cua Ha Tien (Ha Tien thap vinh). Ngay 7/6/1738, Ninh Vuong mat, tho 42 tuoi, o ngoi 13 nam. Sau trieu Nguyen truy ton la Tuc Tong Hieu Ninh Hoang de. Ninh Vuong co 9 nguoi con (3con trai, 6 con gai).

Vo Vuong - Nguyen Phuc Khoat: Nguyen Phuc Khoat (Vo Vuong, 1738-1765) Nguyen Phuc Khoat, sinh nam Giap Ngo (1714), la con truong cua Ninh Vuong duoc len ngoi chua ngay 7/6/1738, lay hieu la Tu Te Dao nhan. Nam Giap Ty (1744), Nguyen Phuc Khoat len ngoi vuong xung la Vo Vuong cho duc an Quoc Vuong. Xuong chieu bo cao thien ha, lay Phu Xuan lam kinh do. Tu nam Giap Tuat (1754), de xung dang voi kinh do cua Nguyen Vuong, Phu Xuan duoc xay dung them hang loat cung dien theo quy mo de vuong. Dac biet chiec ao dai Viet Nam tha thuot xinh dep nhu hien nay, phai trai qua mot qua trinh phat trien, no duoc hinh thanh tu doi Vo Vuong Nguyen Phuc Khoat. Nam 1757, Vo Vuong dat Rach Gia lam dao Kien Giang, Ca Mau lam dao Long Xuyen. Ngay 7/6/1765, Vo Vuong Nguyen Phuc Khoat mat, tho 52 tuoi, noi ngoi duoc 27 nam. Trieu Nguyen truy ton ong laThe tong Hieu vu Hoang de. Vo Vuong co 30 nguoi con (18 con trai, 12 con gai).

Dinh Vuong - Nguyen Phuc Thuan: Nguyen Phuc Thuan (Dinh Vuong, 1765-1777) Nguyen Phuc Thuan sinh ngay 31/12/1753, la con thu 16 cua Nguyen Phuc Khoat. Vo Vuong luc dau lap con thu 9 la Phuc Hieu lam thai tu, nhung Hieu mat som, con trai Hieu la Hoang ton Phuc Duong con tho au ma hoang tu ca la Chuong cung da mat. Hoang tu thu hai la Nguyen Phuc Luan cung rat khoi ngo tuan tu, theo thu tu se phai lap Hoang ton Duong hoac Phuc Luan len ngoi nen da trao Luan cho mot thay hoc noi tieng la Truong Van Hanh day bao. Nhung khi Vo Vuong mat, tinh hinh lai dao nguoc. Quyen than Truong Phuc Loan khong muon lap Phuc Luan vi Luan da lon tuoi, kho be long hanh. Truong Phuc Loan lai chon Nguyen Phuc Thuan moi 12 tuoi len ngoi vua. Phuc Luan khong duoc lap ma con bi bat giam. Truong Van Hanh cung bi giet chet. Nguyen Phuc Thuan con nho tuoi, moi quyen hanh deu do Truong Phuc Loan sap dat. Loan tu phong la Quoc pho. Loan thau tom toan bo tu chinh su den kinh te. Cac nguon loi chu yeu cua vuong quoc Dang Trong deu roi vao tay Truong Phuc Loan va ho hang cua han. Ngay nang, Loan cho dem phoi cua cai quy bau lam sang ruc ca mot goc troi. Co tien, co quyen, Loan mac suc hoanh hanh ngang nguoc, nguoi nguoi ai nay deu oan gian. Nghia quan Tay Son do Nguyen Nhac cam dau o Quy Nhon duoc nhan dan dong tinh ung ho ngay cang lon manh. Them vao do, thang 5 nam Giap Ngo (1774) Chua Trinh lai cho dai quan vao danh Nguyen. Ca nghia quan Tay Son lan quan Trinh deu neu khau hieu : "Tru khu quyen than Truong Phuc Loan va ton pho Hoang ton Duong". Chien tranh loan lac lai no ra, dat Thuan Hoa truoc tru phu la the ma nay tram be xo xac tieu dieu, nguoi chet doi day duong. Truoc tinh canh do, khong co cach nao khac, ton that nha Nguyen cung nhau lap tuc bat troi Truong Phuc Loan nop cho quan Trinh. Thang 12 nam 1974, quan Trinh chiem duoc Phu Xuan va dat quan cai tri Thuan Hoa. Trong so quan lai nha Trinh cu vao tran thu Thuan Hoa co Le Quy Don (1776). Nghia quan Tay Son tim cach hoa hoan voi quan Trinh de yen mat Bac va ranh tay danh Nguyen o phia Nam. Dai quan Tay Son ca thuy lan bo danh vao Gia Dinh. Chua Nguyen Phuc Thuan chay ve Dinh Tuong roi lai chay sang Long Xuyen. Thang 9 nam Dinh Dau (1777), quan Tay Son do Nguyen Hue thong linh danh chiem Long Xuyen, chua Nguyen bi chet tran. Nhu vay Nguyen Phuc Thuan o ngoi chua duoc 12 nam, tho 24 tuoi khong co con noi. Sau trieu Nguyen truy ton la Due Tong Hieu dinh Hoang de. Dinh vuong Nguyen Phuc Thuan chet, ket thuc giai doan lich su cua 9 doi chua Nguyen Dang Trong.

Lat do chua Nguyen: O Dang Trong, trong nhung nam cuoi doi, Chua Nguyen Phuc Khoat dam ra say me tuu sac, khong con quan tam viec nuoc nua, giao het moi viec cho quyen than Truong Phuc Loan.
Chinh su ho Nguyen ngay tu thoi Nguyen Phuc Khoat da gay nhung anh huong tieu cuc cho dan chung. He thong thue khoa phuc tap, cong kenh, nang ne, quan duoi lam thu khien dan phai nop nhieu hon quy dinh.
